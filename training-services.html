Personal Training Services

My approach is centred on your individual needs. I provide a personalised approach to your health and movement, with services meticulously designed to address your unique requirements, helping you overcome limitations and achieve lasting results.

Utilising the Functional Patterns Methodology, my aim is to address the root causes of dysfunction and pain, rather than merely treating symptoms. I will collaborate with you to build a more resilient and efficient body, enabling you to move better and live with reduced discomfort.

  • 1-on-1 Personal Training Sessions: Dedicated guidance focused on your progress.
  • In-depth Postural Assessments: A comprehensive look at your body's alignment.
  • Gait (Walking/Running) Analysis: Optimising your natural movement patterns.
  • Custom Corrective Exercise Programs: Targeted exercises for improved function.
  • Tailored Seniors Personal Training: Specialised programs for the needs of older adults.
  • Flexible Online Coaching Options: Support and accountability, wherever you are.

Special Focus: Seniors Personal Training

Training is adapted for the unique needs of older adults, focusing on improving balance, strength, and the ease of daily activities based on individual fitness levels, limitations, and goals.

A Personal Note: I have the privilege of training my own parents. It's been incredibly rewarding to see their progress firsthand as they experience significant improvements in balance, strength, and their overall ease of movement in daily life.
