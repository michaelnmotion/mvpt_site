About Me

Hi, I'm Michael! My journey into personal training and Functional Patterns started with my own struggles with chronic hip pain, movement limitations and surgery reccomendations. After two years trying numerous conventional approaches without success, I discovered FP and it completely changed my understanding of human movement and biomechanics.

My passion is to help others break free from pain and move better, whether that's for daily life, sport, or longevity. I'm dedicated to continuous learning and applying the most effective methods to help my clients achieve real, sustainable results.
